01 / OriginThe person behind the pixels

I don't pick a lane.
I engineer where the lanes converge.

Most creatives choose between the rational and the romantic. I refuse the trade. My work lives in the seam — where a well-formed React component feels as intentional as a cinematic cut.

I started with code at eighteen, editing funny videos at nineteen, and by twenty I was writing subtitle passes for indie documentaries while shipping web apps for local studios. The threads always braided together.

Today, I run a lean, one-person studio for clients who need a rare combination — someone who can architect the backend, colour-grade the launch trailer, engineer the AI copilot that powers it, and localize the whole thing into three languages before Friday.

If you need a specialist, hire a specialist. If you need someone who sees the whole picture — welcome.

05 / How we workA tight, five-step protocol

Deliberate steps. No surprises.
No mystery invoices.

/ Step 01
Brief & alignment
A 45-minute call. I ask more questions than you expect. You leave with a written brief, a scope, and a fixed quote.
→
/ Step 02
Strategy & direction
Before I touch the tools, I write the plan. Moodboards, prompt drafts, wireframes, or edit outlines — whichever fits.
→
/ Step 03
Build in daylight
You get access to a shared workspace with daily progress logs. No waiting, no wondering, no black boxes.
→
/ Step 04
